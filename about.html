Why Smitty+?

Internships and extracurriculars are an essential component of academic success; Smittcamp scholars are motivated, capable individuals who go on to accomplish great things. We felt that some online infrastructure to support students in their pursuit of their career goals was necessary, prompting us to create Smitty+. Here, scholars can view various opportunities relevant to their course of study, and engage in rigorous programs designed to both promote valuable professional experiences, as well as prepare the next generation of leaders.

Striving for excellence.

Whether in a nanobiology lab or an internship at Capitol Hill, Smittcamp encourages all students to venture out beyond their comfort zone and tap into a rich marketplace of ideas.

Get a jumpstart on your career.

Fellowships provide an excellent way to gain valuable skills, receive phenomenal training, and prepare students for successful careers in their field.
